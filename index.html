COLLECTION NFT META PIRATES

Meta Pirate is a collection of 8888 NFT pirates living in the Ethereum blockchain.

Each piece is a unique piece of 3D art with a collection of over 150 features. Our goal is to create a strong community of ambitions that, like the pirates, are hungry for ambition and ready to conquer the lands & seas of the metaverse with us.

Meta Pirates aspires to a philosophy of conquerors. You will be connected with each member of this crew, all having the same vision of things as you: to plunder the riches of web 3.0

EVERYTHING STARTS HERE...

One day, a pirate who had left to discover new lands, comes back claiming to have found a kind of dimensional portal, which could bring the crew to new lands & seas: the metaverse.

After long investigations and research, the crew learns that untold treasures are to be found in this new world, but that some enemies had already managed to gain access to the gates leading to this piece of paradise.

Excited and bored with the terrestrial continents, the 8,888 pirates decide to lift the ink and go in search of the portals that can teleport them into the metaverse.

The RoadMap

1

Phase 1 : Welcome aboard !

Our priority is to develop an active and cohesive crew to ensure the success of our collection. We want our crew to be made up of pirates who share our values and those of the pirate code. The quality of our designs is also an essential part of our collection, so we have chosen (...) to represent our pirates in the best way.

We will announce our release date as soon as we are satisfied with the quality and the technical parts of the project

2

PHASE 2: Crew privileges and Merchandising

Our NFT holders will be invited to exclusive events in the world of cinema, art, watches, entrepreneurship, fashion and parties.

Meta-pirate crew members will have access to exclusive premium merch. This will not be limited to a simple t-shirt. Each member will have an original piece customized with their NFT number.

3

Phase 3 : HOLD TO EARN

After the reveal, your pirate goes to conquer the blockchain. He will plunder the treasures of the blockchain to bring you between 1 and 3 treasure chests per month.

In these chests are big rewards that depend on the rarity of your Meta Pirates. Among the rewards, you can find etherums, exclusive meta-pirates merch and who knows, maybe even elixirs that can increase the value of your NFT... There are 3 types of treasure: legendary, epic and rare.

4

Phase 4 : Heart of pirates: the soul of the Ocean

Pirates sail on the ocean, so we must take care of it. It is the ocean that allows us to sail, to feed, to conquer. The ocean offers unforgettable moments to all pirates worthy of the name. That's why we want to take care of it. For this, 70,000 dollars will be donated to an association fighting against the pollution of the seas and oceans. The association will be chosen by the DAO.

5

Phase 5 : Play To Earn

Experience the battlefields through your nft! Introduction to the game Meta Pirate P2E where you have the possibility to build your crew and conquer your enemies' lands. PvE and PvP modes as well as a prop market will be available to balance the game economy. Players can monetize their game experiences by looting their enemies' tokens and trading their MPR tokens for Dollars.

6

Phase 6: Let's conquer the oceans of the Metaverse !

We've assembled a crew of determined pirates with a thirst for conquest. Now it's time to sail the oceans of the Metaverse to establish our influence!

To do this, we will make the "Black Chain" available to our crew in order to gather the meta pirate members.

Our galleon will be available in the metaverse and exclusive to our crew members. On this galleon, our crew members will have access to exclusive activities (casino, games), art galleries for our legendary NFT's, hold masterminds on the WeB 3.0 to exchange their latest business opportunities and ideas, as well as have a good time and maintain a tight community.

FAQ

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for "Non-Fungible Token". It's a specific, unique, digital item with blockchain-managed ownership that users can buy, own and trade.

What is Meta Pirates?

Meta Pirates is an NFT collection consisting of 8,888 unique 3D generative art. Owning a Meta Pirates NFT allows you to benefit from opportunities related to the crypto and business world.

When will the official Meta Pirate launch take place?

he official launch date is to exclusively be announced on Discord and our social media. Our plan is to take over the Metaverse by the end of January 2022.

How can I mint?

You can mint exclusively on our website on launch day. We will share the exact date and time on Discord. Join now on Discord!
